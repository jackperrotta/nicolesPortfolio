ABOUT ME

I love telling stories

After the realization set in that writing in my journal wouldn't make me a career, I set out to find the best outlet for my passion of writing. It took me some time, and well, lots of writing, but I eventually figured out that being the voice of a company might just be the right choice. Since finding a way to incorporate my career into my passion, I have had two public relations internships, In House Marketing and Breslow Partners. Currently, I am an employee at Breslow Partners where I have managed several social media accounts, including Hai Street Kitchen & Co. and Genji Sushi Bars. In addition, I take the lead in managing the public relations plan of Phillips Seafood Restaurants, which has locations in three different cities. Although promoting restaurants isn't necessarily my passion, being able to talk about brands I love and believe in is...and the delicious food is a bonus.

Nicole Elizabeth Gormley


In addition to working at Breslow Partners, I am on the executive board of a Philadelphia start-up called Vitris. At Vitris we connect consumers with broken smartphones and tablets to repair shops. We allow customers to compare a list of shops by price, customer rating, and location without having to go through the hassle of endless Google searches and comparisons. Vitris makes getting your phone or tablet repaired quick, easy and convenient. Since joining the team over a year ago as the communications director, I have set up their social media channels and developed a strategic public relations plan. To date, we at Vitris have raised $80,000 in capital, $20,000 coming from Temple University's renowned Be Your Own Boss Bowl, where this past April we placed first in our division. Upon graduating from Temple University in the fall, I hope to continue to my work with Vitris and land a full-time position with a company that allows me to fulfill my desire to write, inspire, and share my ideas!
